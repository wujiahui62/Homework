Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Augueste-Dominique Ingres

The portait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclasscial artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of the three portains of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musèe du Louvre, Paris
